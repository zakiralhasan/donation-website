Oure Donate Plan

We help people in different ways, which are described below in detail.

Please see our working areas and give us feedback if you think.

Medicine for the patient.

We give medicine to a patient who is affected by the disaster. For this reason, we collected donations from you and others to help suffering people.

Blood for the patient.

We collect blood for a patient who is affected by the disaster. Blood is free of cost but has to bear processing costs for blood. For this reason, we collected donations from you.

Clothes for the family

We collect your unnecessary clothes for the affected family. Please donate your unnecessary clothes and also, give money to help others.
